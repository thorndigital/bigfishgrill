Thank you Issaquah!
With sincere gratitude, we thank you all for a wonderful 10 years! It is with a heavy heart that it’s time to say good-bye. The Big Fish Grill in the Issaquah Highlands will be closing at 10pm tonight, Friday March 31st. We have served thousands of guests, celebrated birthdays, anniversaries, holidays and more. These are memories that our family and team will be forever grateful for. We hope you will come enjoy your favorite Big Fish Grill menu items at our Woodinville location. The Woodinville Big Fish Grill will remain open and serve lunch and dinner daily. We look forward to seeing you there!

Sincerely,
The Orrico family and all of us at Big Fish Grill in Issaquah
